Research · Multi-Agent RL

Reinforcement Learning CTF— Multi-Agent AI

A PPO-based multi-agent reinforcement learning system trained to play autonomous surface vessel Capture-the-Flag in cooperative–competitive teams.

The system uses curriculum learning to scale agents from 2v2 up to 8v8 engagements, and league-style opponent training — where past policy snapshots are sampled by ELO rating — to encourage policy diversity and prevent strategic collapse. Trained policies were evaluated for robustness against both scripted heuristic baselines and held-out adversarial snapshots from outside the league.

  • ELO Selection
